Ice Cream Cookie Sandwich Recipe

Description

Craving something sweet? This Ice Cream Cookie Sandwich recipe will be the yummiest treat you've eaten this year.

Ingredients

  • 75g butter, softened
  • 40g light brown soft sugar
  • 40g caster sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 egg
  • 150g plain flour
  • 1/4 tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • 100g plan chocolate chips
  • 4 scoops vanilla ice cream
